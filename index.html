About Turgeon.Frames

Turgeon.Frames is all about versatility — capturing life from every angle. Whether it’s fast-paced sports, street photography, portraits, events, or content creation, Gabriel Turgeon brings a creative eye and adaptable style to every frame. No matter the vibe, the goal stays the same: clean, authentic visuals that speak louder than words.

Gab’s not just behind the camera — he’s also a hybrid worker. So if you ever need editing, retouching, or post-production magic, he’s your guy. From raw shots to the final polish, Turgeon.Frames has you covered.

~ SERVICES/PRICES ~

At Turgeon.Frames, it’s all about capturing moments that matter. Whether it’s for a shoot, event, or creative project, the goal is to deliver visuals that hit — clean, real, and full of personality. Every service is flexible and tailored to your vibe, with a mix of creativity, quality, and care behind every shot.
Note: Prices listed are for 1 hour sessions and may vary depending on the event or project details.

Photography Sessions

Each session is built around you — your vibe, your energy, and your style. Whether it's laid-back, bold, creative, or classic, the goal is to capture you in the most real and striking way possible. Prices start at $90 for a single shoot and $150 for 2 or more people that comes with edited photos, and a private online gallery for easy sharing and downloading.

Videography Sessions

Capture moments that move. From storytelling edits to cinematic visuals, every video is crafted to bring your vision to life — whether it’s for a brand, an event, or just a memory you want to relive in motion. Prices start at $100 per project, which includes a fully edited video with clean cuts, color grading, synced audio, and a private online link for easy sharing and downloading.

Hybrid Sessions

Photos freeze a moment. Videos bring it to life. At turgeon.frames, you get the best of both. Whether it’s a full event, a personal project, or content for your brand, hybrid sessions offer a mix of high-quality photography and cinematic videography — all tailored to your style. Prices start at $120 for a hybrid bundle, which includes a full photo shoot, an edited video, edited photos, and a private online gallery and video link for easy sharing and downloading.
